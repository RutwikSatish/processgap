The Problem
Every company knows which processes feel slow. Nobody knows which step costs the most.
Process mining tools exist. They cost $80K–$500K to implement. Management consultants charge $200K for the same analysis. Companies between $5M and $500M in revenue have nothing. The same bottlenecks persist for years because nobody quantified them well enough to justify the fix.

The Solution
ProcessGap runs the same analysis in 10 minutes.
Map your process steps, enter cycle times and resource costs, classify each step as VA / NNVA / NVA. ProcessGap computes utilization at every step using Little's Law, identifies the binding constraint, quantifies the annual waste cost, and models the ROI of fixing each bottleneck.

No consultants. No six-figure software license. No six-week implementation project.

Five-Step Analysis

The method, not the marketing.

01
Map Your Process
Enter each step with cycle time, wait time, resources, and cost. Classify as VA, NNVA, or NVA per VSM conventions.
VSM
02
VSM Analysis
Calculates total cycle time, value-added ratio, and process efficiency. Computes annual waste cost per step in dollars.
Six Sigma DMAIC
03
Bottleneck Detection
Utilization analysis via Little's Law identifies the step where demand exceeds capacity — the true constraint, not a guess.
Little's Law
04
ROI Simulation
Models the annual dollar value of fixing each bottleneck. Conservative recovery estimate based on current waste rate.
ROI Modeling
